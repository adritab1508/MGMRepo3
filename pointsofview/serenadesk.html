The Register

Serena decides to see if there is something peculiar about the in-times and out-times of everyone involved.

Swati returned that night at 8:00 PM. Probably from work.

Ruchi came back from the library at 6:00 PM and was in her room until the power outtage, presumably.

And Lakshmi Akka had left for an hour in the morning. Serena had no idea why.

The Voice Mail

Sometimes, when she cannot quite wrap her head around Vicky's death and how she feels about it, Serena listens to the last voice message he sent on the hostel landline. A message to her, for some reason, even though she was not supposed to be there tonight had it not been for his death.
